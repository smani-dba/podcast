About Our Series

The Modern Data Playbook Series is a platform for IT and data leaders to share their experiences in transforming raw data into actionable insights that drive business growth. Our series features candid conversations with professionals who are navigating the challenges and opportunities of data transformation.

This series is brought to you by QDataX, LLC. We specialize in helping small and medium-sized businesses with SQL Server modernize their analytics with Microsoft Fabric. We believe that every business, regardless of size, deserves access to a modern data platform to achieve its goals.

We help our clients achieve outcomes such as increasing profitability, reducing expenses, and accelerating revenue growth by turning their data into their most valuable asset.
